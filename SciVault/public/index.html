About SciVault

SciVault is a centralized repository for managing biological research data. From genomic sequences to microscopy images, SciVault allows researchers, students, and institutions to easily upload, explore, and categorize research files.

Founded in 2025 by a team of ENTHUSIASTIC students, SciVault is dedicated to supporting open science initiatives and fostering cross-disciplinary collaboration. Our platform ensures data integrity, versioning, and secure access control, making it the trusted choice for universities, research labs, and independent scientists.
